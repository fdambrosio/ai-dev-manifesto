# AI Dev Manifesto

## About
This manifesto originated from Francesco D'Ambrosio within the Minsait-Indra team development group.

The manifesto outlines principles and values for the development of AI technologies, focusing on ethical considerations, collaboration, and innovation.

## Principles
- Focus on ethical AI development
- Encourage collaboration among diverse teams
- Strive for transparency in AI processes

## Values
- Accountability
- Trustworthiness
- Inclusiveness

## Conclusion
Together, we will shape the future of AI development responsibly.
