# Contributing to the AI Dev Manifesto

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the AI Dev Manifesto. We welcome translations, improvements, and any kind of contributions that enhance the quality and accessibility of the manifesto.

## Guidelines for Contributing

### Translations
1. **Language Assurance**: Ensure you are fluent in both the source and target languages.
2. **Culturally Relevant**: Be mindful of cultural nuances in your translations.
3. **Use of Terminology**: Maintain consistency in terms of key concepts. Refer to existing translations if available.

### Improvements
1. **Clarity and Precision**: Aim to improve the clarity of the text where possible. 
2. **Suggestions**: Submit suggestions for enhancements or alternative phrasing in sections that may benefit from it.
3. **Content Requests**: If you identify areas that are lacking in detail or require clarification, feel free to propose additional content.

### Process
1. **Fork** the repository and create a new branch for your contribution.
2. Make your changes and commit them with a clear message describing your contribution.
3. **Submit a Pull Request** detailing your changes, the reasons for them, and any relevant discussions or references.

### Code of Conduct
By participating in this project, you agree to abide by the code of conduct and maintain a respectful and collaborative atmosphere.

## Thank You!
Your contributions help make the AI Dev Manifesto a living document that reflects the shared knowledge and insights of the community!
